Privacy Policy for GridLog

Last Updated: June 16, 2026

Cream City Soft operates the GridLog mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

1. Information Collection and Use

GridLog is designed as a personal ledger for delivery drivers. We collect information manually input by you (earnings, mileage, tags) to calculate profitability.

2. Location Data (Point-in-Time Snapshots)

For Premium Tier users, we use GPS to capture location only at the moment you manually Accept or Reject an offer. This is used solely to generate your Earnings Heatmap. We do not track your location in the background or monitor your route continuously.

3. Data Storage

All job history and location snapshots are stored locally on your device. Currently, GridLog does not upload your personal data to external servers.

4. Billing

Subscriptions are handled via the Google Play Billing Library. We do not store your financial information.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions, contact us at: creamcitysoftware@gmail.com
